MEDIA PRODUCTION

Ok, so hear me; I still may not be great at holding a camera, BUT I've gotten pretty good at developing a narrative. Here are a few short films that I scripted, storyboared, edited, and filmed (with some help on the filming part) as projects for courses, Intro To Media Production (2020) & Non-Linear Video Editing (2022).

Kinship Legal Guardianship is an informative film explaining the process to legal guardianship.

'Cause That's What I Do is a feature film that tells a fictional story.

Can't Escape The Mischief is an feature film that tells a story of my quarantine experience during the COVID-19 pandemic.
