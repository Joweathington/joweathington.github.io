Ok, so I still may not be great at holding a camera, but I've gotten pretty good at developing a narrative. Kinship Legal Guardianship -- an informative film explaining the process to legal guardianship.

'Cause That's What I Do -- a feature film that tells a fictional story.

Can't Escape The Mischief -- a feature film that tells a story of my quarantine experience during the COVID-19 pandemic.
