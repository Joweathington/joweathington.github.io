UALR TRIumph Pilot Program 2024 - 2025

A collaborative workshop series between UALR and UAMS in which UAMS research scholars completed the TRIumph training course to develop their public speaking skills. The course was led by Julien Mirivel, Ph.D., from the University of Arkansas at Little Rock, and focused on how presenters organize material and present their research using delivery techniques such as voice, eye contact, and movement that foster audience connection.
